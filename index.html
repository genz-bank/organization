GenZ Business Model Canvas

The Business Model Canvas is a strategic management template used for developing GenZ business model and documenting. It offers a visual chart with elements describing a firm's value proposition, infrastructure, customers, and finances, assisting businesses to align their activities by illustrating potential trade-offs.

Cost Structure

The Cost Structure building block of the Business Model Canvas details the monetary cost of operating as the GenZ business. Cost structure represents all the costs the GenZ business will incur under the business model, especially those costs to maintain the key resources that make up the core business model. In this section we will describe cost structure items.

Roles: IT, SPO, CTO, CEO

Organizational roles are user groups that represent company units, departments or subdivisions in the organizational structure, for example, the “IT” or the “Stake Pool Operator”. Many roles are considerd in GenZ Bank to setup, maintain and manage staking pools. Number of emplyees depend on how many pools we suport. These emplyees effects to the organization cost directly.

Infrastructure & Resources

Information technology infrastructure, or IT infrastructure, refers to the combined components needed for the operation and management of enterprise IT services and IT environments. Depends on networks supported, we need infrastructure and resources which effects our cost.

Read more about Infrastructure

Netwokrs

A blockchain network is a technical infrastructure that provides ledger and smart contract (chaincode) services to applications. To setup a node needs assets and resources. So, supported networks effect costs directly. Networks, such as Tron, Avalanch and Cardano are analyzed by GenZ.

Revenue Stream

In the Business Model Canvas, the Revenue Streams component encompasses the money that the company generates with each previously defined Customer Segment. But that does not mean the “profit” earned, but the revenue flow involved.

Staking

Staking is when you lock crypto assets for a set period of time to help support the operation of a blockchain. In return for staking your crypto, you earn more cryptocurrency. The GenZ company hase to stake a minimum value on nodes, so this cause a revenue stream. APR shows how much we earn. For more information see networks.
